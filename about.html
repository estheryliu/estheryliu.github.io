Hi! It's me, Esther (she/her/hers). If you can't find me watercoloring or playing my oboe, catch me outside admiring a koi pond.

I am currently a third year student at the University of California, Santa Barbara. My fields of study are Communication and Music. I am also pursuing the Multimedia track under the Professional Writing Minor.

Though a bit of a stationery addict, I take great pride in all the mini crafting endeavors I experiment with. Translating a variety of artistic skills through numerous solo and collaborative projects, I'm looking to apply myself in the visual communications and marketing field.

This site serves as my online portfolio, hosting a collection of writing and design samples. Please feel free to poke around and share your thoughts with me below!
